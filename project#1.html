    Introduction:
  • A Song of Ice and fire is a series of epic fantasy novels by the American novelist and screenwriter George R. R. Martin.
  • He began the first volume of the series, A Game of Thrones, in 1991, and it was published in 1996.
  • Martin, who initially envisioned the series as a trilogy, has published five out of a planned seven volumes.
  • The fifth and most recent volume of the series, A Dance with Dragons, was published in 2011 and took Martin six years to write.
  • He is currently writing the sixth novel,The Winds of Winter.A seventh novel A Dream of Spring is planned.


Brief:
  • A Song of Ice and Fire takes place on the fictional continents Westeros and Essos.
  • The books have sold 90 million copies worldwide as of April 2019,after having been translated into 47 languages as of January 2017.
